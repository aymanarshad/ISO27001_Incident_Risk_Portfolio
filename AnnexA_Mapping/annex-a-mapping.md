# Annex A Control Mapping – Phishing Incident

## Control A.6.3 – Information Security Awareness & Training
Applicable due to phishing incident caused by lack of user awareness.

## Control A.8.23 – Email and Web Security
Applicable as email was the attack vector used for credential harvesting.

## Control A.5.17 – Authentication Information
Applicable due to credential compromise and lack of MFA enforcement.

## Control A.5.25 – Incident Response Management
Applicable to ensure incidents are handled, documented, and reviewed.
